## 4.4.2 Lambda Shipper Test

### Objective

Validate the log ingestion flow from CloudWatch to the processing system via Lambda Shipper.

---

---

### Workflow Description

- Logs are generated by the system and sent to CloudWatch  
- Lambda Shipper is triggered to process the logs  
- Data is forwarded to SQS  
- Lambda Processor continues processing the data  
- Results are stored in S3 and DynamoDB, and notifications are sent via email  

---

### Conclusion

The ingestion pipeline operates reliably, ensuring that logs are correctly transferred from CloudWatch to the processing system.
